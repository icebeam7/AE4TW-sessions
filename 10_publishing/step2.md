## Step 2. Create an account 

1. Visit [Vercel website](https://vercel.com) and sign up for a Vercel free account.

<img width="1212" height="656" alt="Screenshot 2026-04-23 at 13 49 52" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ab5d6216-8f2e-49d7-89be-a4c85c8142c5" />

2. Select "I'm working on personal projects (Hobby)" which is the free option.

<img width="575" height="648" alt="Screenshot 2026-04-23 at 13 50 14" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7dd93516-da0c-433a-b8ec-6769060c6b7b" />

3. I recommend you to select "Continue with GitHub" so a connection between your repository and the deployment service is set up as part of the process.

<img width="575" height="660" alt="Screenshot 2026-04-23 at 13 50 31" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/52744888-2477-4e5d-a5e0-58514434af5f" />

4. You will be asked to sign in to GitHub.

<img width="511" height="660" alt="Screenshot 2026-04-23 at 13 51 20" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/efa96e52-05ed-4b01-ae41-ea96ca488c77" />

5. Authorize Vercel so it can be used with your GitHub account.

<img width="544" height="771" alt="Screenshot 2026-04-23 at 13 52 02" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/598559f4-02b3-4699-9573-3425b96fc396" />

6. After you confirm your account (you might need to provide your mobile phone to get an activation code), click on Continue with GitHub. 

<img width="584" height="476" alt="Screenshot 2026-05-11 at 12 16 22" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/379684ba-04cd-43c3-98d7-58ee0acc0b80" />

7. Click on Install.

<img width="584" height="476" alt="Screenshot 2026-05-11 at 12 17 52" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/fbfa9b75-07d9-41a7-b9b8-45160490be7b" />

8. Select your account.

<img width="686" height="379" alt="Screenshot 2026-05-11 at 12 23 51" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/86f26318-f8a8-47b6-9cdb-96bf8b5dd674" />

9. Select all repositories or choose a specific repository where you have your code. Then click on Install.

<img width="686" height="804" alt="Screenshot 2026-05-11 at 12 25 06" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ee1176de-b487-4edb-92b7-2607e51b89f9" />
