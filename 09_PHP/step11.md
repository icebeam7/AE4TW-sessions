## Step 11. Test the registration page (Part 2 – Edit an existing employee)

1.	In the Employee List Page, click on the edit icon (pencil) of an employee to edit it. 

<img width="2172" height="1152" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/132d5a59-5a55-43d8-a660-5a0ff3627f99" />

2.	You will see the employee’s data in the registration page. Set a different number, department, salary, and hire date. Then, click on the Register Employee button.

<img width="2172" height="1016" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b4f9fa2e-c0fb-4b48-920d-f3851bb7d1a5" />
